History of Food Blogging

With the rise of technology with the younger generation, people have been becoming more clever with what they put out in the social media world. Food has become a main attraction that can be a common thread for people all around the world. With outlets like Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, it is just a click away from showing everyone what you ate that day. There are food blogs in a website form, with fun recipes for families or how to keep fit with healthy foods, or food instagrams where users post a delicious dish that they made or ate at a popular restaurant. Currently, half of surveyed customers learn about food through social media. 9% have downloaded a mobile food app in the last year
